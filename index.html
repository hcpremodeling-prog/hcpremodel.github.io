About HCP Remodeling

23 Years.
One Standard.

HCP Remodeling has been serving the Greater Houston area for over 23 years. Every project is managed personally by Benny Honorat, from the initial estimate through the final walkthrough.

We do not use subcontractors. Our crew handles tile, carpentry, plumbing, electrical, flooring, and finish work in-house, which means one point of contact, consistent quality, and no coordination gaps.

We back every project with a two-year workmanship warranty and we do not consider a job finished until it is right.
